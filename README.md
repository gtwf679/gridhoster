## What to Keep in Mind

GridHoster doesn't have a massive review footprint online. It's not Hostinger or Bluehost with thousands of user reviews across multiple platforms. What reviews do exist are mixed in terms of volume, so if you're the type who reads 200 user opinions before making a decision, you'll find slimmer pickings here.

Their VPS offering doesn't come with Windows — it's Linux only. If you need Windows Server, look elsewhere.

Customer support is 24/7/365, but their primary channel appears to be ticketing rather than live chat. For most routine hosting questions that's fine, but if you need immediate help at 2am, ticket response times are something to consider.
